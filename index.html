What I do

Many roots,
one direction.

Take what fits your moment. Each of these can be a starting point or a whole journey.

Coaching & Strategy

For when the direction isn't clear. I work with leaders and founders to find clarity, prioritize what matters, and decide what to stop doing.

Team & Culture

For when the team doesn't work like a team. Communication, shared responsibility, leadership distribution, and trust — without the jargon.

Flow & Decisions

For when things get stuck. Simple systems for delegation, prioritization, and getting work out the door — built around the people doing it.

AI with Responsibility

AI works best when people understand what it can and can't do. I help teams adopt it with intention — asking the right questions before picking the tools.

Facilitation & Training

Workshops, talks, and training that make people think. Available for events, organizations, and ongoing programs. In Portuguese and English.

Story

Brazilian roots,
European branches.

I grew up in Brazil and built my career across fintech, healthcare, and tech — always at the intersection of people, strategy, and organizational change.

Over 12 years I've worked with teams in hypergrowth, post-merger integration, and large-scale organizational change. I've seen what happens when organizations force growth. I help them let it happen instead.

Today I'm based in Hamburg, working across Europe and Latin America — and still learning something new every cycle.
